Approval Samplers#

We present below the validation tests we ran for approval samplers.

Impartial#

prefsampling.approval.impartial()

In the impartial model every candidate has the same probability p of being approved. Thus, the probability of generating an approval ballot of length k follows a binomial distribution of parameters p and k.

Resampling#

prefsampling.approval.resampling()

See the work of Szufa et al. (2022) for more details on this model.

Disjoint Resampling#

prefsampling.approval.disjoint_resampling()

See the work of Szufa et al. (2022) for more details on this model.

Noise#

prefsampling.approval.noise()

The probability distribution generated by the noise model based on the Hamming distance.

Identity#

prefsampling.approval.identity()

The identity sampler only samples a single ballot. This is what we observe here.
