Citation Policy

Citation Policy#

Developing and maintaining such package represents a lot of work. If you are using our code we ask you to cite to credit our work. You can do so by citing the Guide to Numerical Experiments on Elections in Computational Social Choice (this package being one its component of it).

Boehmer N., Faliszewski P., Janeczko Ł., Kaczmarczyk A., Lisowski G., Pierczyński G., Rey S.,
Stolicki D., Szufa S., Wąs T. (2024).
Guide to Numerical Experiments on Elections in Computational Social Choice.
33rd International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence: Survey Track.
